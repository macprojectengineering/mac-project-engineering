ℹ️ About

MAC Project Engineering LLP is an international consulting firm specialising in intellectual services for IP network and telecommunications infrastructures.

We work with carriers, enterprises, and system integrators worldwide — across Europe, the United States, the Middle East, and North Africa — to design, deploy, and operate complex IP networks in heterogeneous multi-vendor environments.

Our expertise spans the entire network stack: from Layer 2 switching to perimeter security (Stormshield, Fortigate), NMS supervision (SNMPv3, SSHv2, Syslog), and advanced resilience protocols (STP/RSTP/MSTP, REP, ERP G.8032, Turbo Ring). All engagements follow an exclusive B2B model with direct client relationships and no intermediaries.

🔄 Network resilience protocols

IEEE 802.1D/w/s
STP · RSTP · MSTP
Classic Spanning Tree, Rapid STP for accelerated convergence, and Multiple STP for multi-VLAN management. Layer 2 loop prevention in meshed topologies.
🏭 Cisco, Juniper, Huawei, Hirschmann, Moxa
Cisco proprietary
REP — Resilient Ethernet Protocol
Cisco STP alternative for ring topologies. Convergence <50 ms, alternate port control — ideal for distribution and access networks.
🏭 Cisco (Catalyst, IE Series)
ITU-T G.8032
ERP — Ethernet Ring Protection
ITU-T standard for Ethernet ring protection. Failover <50 ms, multi-ring support. Widely deployed in carrier and metro Ethernet networks.
🏭 Alcatel, Huawei, Juniper, Hirschmann
Moxa proprietary
Turbo Ring · Turbo Chain
Moxa industrial protocols with ultra-fast convergence <20 ms. Designed for critical environments: energy, transport, industrial automation.
🏭 Moxa (industrial switches)
